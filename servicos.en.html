Commissioning of medium and high voltage substations

Commissioning is the last activity in a substation to ensure the performance and operational safety of high and medium voltage installations. Intec, always aiming for excellence, has specialized professionals and high-tech instruments to carry out commissioning for the following stages:


  • Visual Inspection;
  • Equipment testing;
  • Wiring certification;
  • Functional test;
  • Current and potential injection;
  • Verification of the performance of all commands, signaling and protection, in addition to current and potential circuits;
  • Grounding mesh tests;
  • As Built (supply of projects with the necessary changes that occurred during commissioning);
  • Technical report;
