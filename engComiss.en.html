Electrical tests

Relay parameterization

Preparation and Configuration

Initial configuration of the relay according to technical specifications and desired protection parameters. Preparation of the current injection case, configuring it to simulate the specified electrical conditions.

Current Injection and Testing

Injection of test currents into the relay to verify responses to configured parameters, including overcurrents and fault conditions. Monitoring and adjustment of relay actuation values ​​to ensure accurate and quick response in cases of abnormalities.

Analysis and Adjustments

Evaluation of test results to identify the need for adjustments to the relay parameters. Performing fine adjustments to optimize relay performance and reliability.

Documentation and Final Report

Complete documentation of all tests performed, including settings, measured values ​​and adjustments made. Preparation of a final report validating the correct parameterization and functioning of the relay.
